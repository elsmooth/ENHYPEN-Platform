ENHYPEN is a prominent South Korean boy band formed by BELIFT LAB, a label under HYBE Corporation. The group was established through the 2020 survival competition show I-LAND and officially debuted on November 30, 2020, with their first EP, Border: Day One.  
Name and Fandom
Name Meaning: The name "ENHYPEN" is derived from the hyphen symbol (-). Just as a hyphen connects different words to create new meanings, the name signifies the members' goal to "connect, discover, and grow together to form a new act".  
Fandom Name: Their fans are known as ENGENE, a combination of "engine" (the power that helps them grow) and "gene" (sharing the same DNA to connect and grow together).  
### Current Members
Following the departure of member Heeseung in March 2026, the group consists of six members:  
Jungwon: The group's leader.
Jay: A Korean-American member known for being a mood maker.  
Jake: Known for his Australian background and role in English-language interviews and narrations.  
Sunghoon: A former competitive figure skater noted for his visuals.  
Sunoo: Known for his bubbly personality and distinct "fox-like" eye shape.  
Ni-ki: The youngest member (maknae) and main dancer, originally from Japan.  
### Style and Identity
ENHYPEN is widely recognized for their dark fantasy and vampire-inspired concepts, cinematic storytelling, and high-intensity, synchronized performances.
